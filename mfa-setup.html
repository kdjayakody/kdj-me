Security Tips

Keep your account secure with these best practices.

  • Use a strong password: Your password should be at least 12 characters long and include a mix of uppercase letters, lowercase letters, numbers, and special characters.
  • Enable two-factor authentication: This adds an extra layer of security to your account by requiring a verification code in addition to your password.
  • Don't reuse passwords: Use a unique password for your KDJ account that you don't use for any other service.
  • Keep your device secure: Make sure your device has up-to-date security software and that you keep your operating system and browser updated.
  • Be cautious with links: KDJ will never send you emails with suspicious links or ask for your password. Always check the URL before entering your credentials.
